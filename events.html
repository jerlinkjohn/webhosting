Golden jubilee of Priestly ordination

Fr. Chacko Kakkanattu SDB

Saturday, December 30th, 2017 | XII Apostles church, Chemmalamattom

Fr. Chacko left home in 1951 to become a Salesian missionary. He was ordinated on 9th April, 1967 from Most Reverend Dr. Stephan Fernando at Don Bosco, Shillong, Assam. He was destined to spend his vocation for the souls in the North-East of India. Filling himself with rays of eternal sun; Fr. Chacko become the beacon of light to diverse group of peoples. His great deeds did not limit to just evangelization, but reaching out to several isolated communities in the region and imparting eternal light to their lives; socially, economically, politically and most of all, spiritually.

Kakkanattu

Kakkanattu family is an ancient Christian family hailed from the greenish land of Thidanadu at Kottayam district of Kerala state. Kakkanattu family is proud of sharing its long history and heritage to entire world. This website is a small step in this regard.
